About us

Jinwanda (金湾达) is located in Shengze (盛泽), which is considered the "Silk Capital" of China, and is about an hour driving from Shanghai.

Our expertise lies in the global textile market and we boast a comprehensive production chain, from production to distribution.

Our factory is around 2500m2 and we have more than 300 advanced water jet looms. Our factory is also equipped with a full testing lab for fabric inspection. The daily output is around 100km of thread.

Our products are used in garments, outdoor equipment, medical protection. We distribute our products to China, Southeast Asia, Europe and North America. Some of our customers include C&A, ZARA, Decathlon, H&M, Sportsmaster, BAON, SAVAGE, and Kidswear.

Currently, our best seller are Nylon Taffeta, Poly Taffeta, Cotton-Poly, Cotton-Nylon, Memory Foam, Real Silk, T800, and 2-way Elastic. Many of the products are in bulk stock and have more than 500 color choices.

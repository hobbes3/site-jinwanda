About Jinwanda (金湾达)

Headquartered in Shengze, China's historic "Silk Capital," Jinwanda is strategically located just an hour's drive from Shanghai. We are a fully integrated textile manufacturer and distributor with a robust global footprint, specializing in high-performance materials for apparel, outdoor equipment, and medical protection.

Our state-of-the-art 2,500 m2 facility houses over 300 advanced water-jet looms and a comprehensive, in-house testing laboratory dedicated to rigorous quality control and fabric inspection. With a daily output exceeding 100 kilometers of technical textiles, we offer the scale and reliability required by the world's leading brands.

Jinwanda proudly serves top-tier global retailers and brands, including ZARA, C&A, H&M, Decathlon, Sportsmaster, BAON, SAVAGE, and Kidswear, distributing across China, Southeast Asia, Europe, and North America.

Core Offerings & In-Stock Program:
Our premium, high-demand product portfolio includes nylon taffeta, polyester taffeta, poly-cotton, nylon-cotton, memory fabric, silk, T800, and 2-way stretch elastic. To support our clients' agile supply chain needs, we maintain extensive bulk inventory with over 500 colorways available for immediate distribution.
